Indian Recipes

Recipe 1: Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, and salt. Let it sit for 1 hour.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until it turns golden brown.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook for a few minutes.
  4. Add cream and simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Serve hot with naan or rice.

Recipe 2: Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, beans, potatoes)
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tsp biryani masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 4 cups water
  • 4 tbsp ghee
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a large pot and saut√© the sliced onion until golden brown.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft.
  4. Add the mixed vegetables, biryani masala, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  5. Add the soaked rice and water. Bring it to a boil, then cover and simmer until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  6. Fluff the rice gently with a fork and serve hot.

Recipe 3: Palak Paneer

Ingredients:

  • 200g paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • 2 cups spinach, blanched and pureed
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a pan and fry the paneer cubes until golden brown. Set them aside.
  2. In the same pan, add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add the chopped onion and cook until it turns translucent.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric powder, garam masala, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add the spinach puree and mix well. Cook for a few minutes.
  7. Add the fried paneer cubes and simmer for 5 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with roti or rice.

Recipe 4: Masoor Dal

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup masoor dal (red lentils)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 4 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the masoor dal and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat ghee in a pressure cooker and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add the chopped onion and cook until it turns golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook until the tomatoes turn soft.
  6. Add the soaked dal and water. Pressure cook for about 3-4 whistles.
  7. Once the pressure is released, open the cooker and mix the dal well.
  8. Serve hot with rice or roti.
