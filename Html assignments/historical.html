Famous Historical Battles

Battle of Thermopylae

Date: 480 BC

An ancient Greek battle fought between an alliance of Greek city-states, led by King Leonidas I of Sparta, and the Persian Empire, led by King Xerxes I. Despite being heavily outnumbered, the Greeks displayed great bravery and held off the Persian forces for several days.

Battle of Hastings

Date: October 14, 1066

A pivotal battle in English history between the Norman-French army, led by William the Conqueror, and the Anglo-Saxon army, led by Harold II. The battle resulted in the Norman conquest of England and the subsequent Norman rule.

Battle of Gettysburg

Date: July 1-3, 1863

A major battle of the American Civil War fought between the Confederate Army of Northern Virginia, commanded by General Robert E. Lee, and the Union Army of the Potomac, led by General George G. Meade. The battle resulted in a Union victory and is considered a turning point in the war.

Battle of Stalingrad

Date: August 23, 1942 - February 2, 1943

A critical battle on the Eastern Front of World War II, where the Soviet Union successfully defended the city of Stalingrad against the German forces. The battle resulted in a decisive Soviet victory and marked a turning point in the war.
