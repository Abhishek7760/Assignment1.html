Landmark Places

  • Taj Mahal

    Location: Agra, India

    An iconic white marble mausoleum, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife, Mumtaz Mahal.

  • Great Wall of China

    Location: Northern China

    An ancient fortification stretching thousands of miles, built to protect China from invasions.

  • Statue of Liberty

    Location: New York City, USA

    A colossal neoclassical statue located on Liberty Island, representing freedom and democracy.

  • Pyramids of Giza

    Location: Giza, Egypt

    An ancient complex of three pyramids, including the Great Pyramid of Giza, built as tombs for the pharaohs.

  • Colosseum

    Location: Rome, Italy

    An amphitheater known for its architectural grandeur, used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles in ancient Rome.
